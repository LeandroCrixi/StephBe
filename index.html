My Story

I work at the intersection of leadership, systems and performance — where complexity, pressure and people collide

My work is grounded in a deep understanding of behaviour inside complex systems. Over time, I’ve focused my research and practice on helping organisations navigate people-related challenges with rigour and realism — aligning leadership behaviour with strategic ambition.
